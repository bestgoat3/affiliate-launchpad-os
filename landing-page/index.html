Frequently Asked Questions

How much time do I need to commit each week? +
Most members train 3–4 days per week, 45–60 minutes per session. Weekly check-ins with Karl run 20–30 minutes. Beyond that, the plan is designed to integrate into your existing schedule — not replace it. If you can carve out 4–5 hours per week total, you have enough time to complete this program successfully.
Do I need a gym, or can I train at home? +
Your training plan is built around whatever equipment you have access to. Gym members get a fully periodized barbell program. Home trainees get a plan that maximizes results with dumbbells, bands, or bodyweight. Karl will build your program during the onboarding call based on exactly what you have available.
How soon will I see visible changes? +
Most members report noticeable changes within 21 days — clothes fitting differently, more energy, improved sleep. Visible physical changes (photos, measurements) are typically clear by week 4–6. The 90-day program is designed to produce a transformation that's unmistakable to you and everyone around you by the end.
What if I have an injury or physical limitation? +
Injuries and limitations are part of your onboarding conversation. Karl has coached members with chronic back issues, post-surgical restrictions, joint conditions, and more. Your plan will be adapted around what's safe and effective for your body specifically. We do require medical clearance for certain conditions — your onboarding call will surface anything that needs it.
Is this a course or actual coaching? +
This is actual coaching — not a video course you watch alone and never revisit. You have a real coach (Karl), a real check-in schedule, a real plan that gets updated based on your real results. There are no pre-recorded modules that do the work for you. The coaching relationship is the product.
What does it cost, and is there a guarantee? +
Investment is discussed on your strategy call after your application is reviewed — it's personalized based on program structure and your specific situation. What we can tell you: it's a premium investment reflecting genuine 1-on-1 coaching, not a commodity digital product. As for results, we stand behind the program — if you show up and do the work, we'll make sure you get your transformation or we continue working with you until you do.
