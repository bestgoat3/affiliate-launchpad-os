Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before applying.

How much money do I need to get started?
The beauty of TikTok Shop affiliate marketing is that you don't need inventory, a website, or a big ad budget. You create content promoting other brands' products and earn a commission on every sale you drive. Your only real startup costs are a smartphone and your time. We recommend having a small reserve of $100–$300 for optional tools, but it's not required to begin.
Do I need to show my face on TikTok?
Face-on-camera content consistently converts best, and we're honest about that. We'll teach you exactly how to build comfort in front of a camera — most students who were terrified on day one become natural in weeks. That said, we do cover faceless content strategies as a supplemental approach. However, if you're absolutely unwilling to ever appear on camera, this program may not be the right fit.
How long until I see my first sale?
Our average student sees their first sale within 2–4 weeks of starting the program. Some see it faster — we've had students make their first sale within 72 hours of launching. TikTok's algorithm rewards consistency, so the more you show up early, the faster the momentum builds. Results depend on your effort, niche choice, and consistency with the framework.
What if I have no experience with affiliate marketing?
Zero experience is actually ideal. You haven't developed bad habits and you're not unlearning failed strategies. Our entire curriculum is built from the ground up for beginners — you'll get step-by-step walkthroughs for account setup, product selection, content creation, and commission optimization. The majority of our students had never made a dollar online before joining.
Is this a course or a coaching program?
It's a live coaching program — not a pre-recorded course you watch alone and forget about. You get weekly live calls with Karl, direct access to him and our coaches in the private community, a personal onboarding call, and real accountability from day one. The curriculum modules are there to support your implementation, not replace human guidance.
What's the investment?
Pricing is discussed on the application call — we don't post it publicly because we customize payment plans based on each student's situation. What we can tell you is that we offer flexible payment options and the investment is a fraction of what one strong month of commissions will generate. Apply first, and we'll walk you through everything on the call.
