About This Policy

Noble Transformation LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates nobletransformation.com and provides premium body recomposition coaching services. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using our website or submitting an application, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

When you submit an application or contact form on this site, we collect the following personal information you voluntarily provide:

  • Full Name — to personalize communications and coaching records
  • Email Address — to send program information, updates, and follow-up messages
  • Phone Number — to contact you via SMS and/or phone regarding your application and our coaching program
  • Application Responses — details you share about your background, goals, and fitness history

We may also automatically collect standard technical information when you visit our site, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs, using cookies and analytics tools. This data is used to improve site performance and is not linked to your personal identity.

How We Use Your Information

The personal information you provide is used to:

  • Review and process your coaching program application
  • Contact you about your application status, next steps, and scheduling
  • Deliver educational content, program updates, and promotional communications related to body recomposition coaching services offered by Noble Transformation LLC
  • Send SMS text messages as described below
  • Conduct scheduled strategy calls and provide ongoing coaching support
  • Improve our website, marketing, and service offerings
SMS / Text Message Consent & Disclosure

By submitting an application or any contact form on this site, you expressly consent to receive SMS (text) messages from Noble Transformation LLC. These messages may include:

  • Application follow-up messages and status updates
  • Reminders for scheduled strategy calls or coaching sessions
  • Program information, coaching updates, and onboarding instructions
  • Promotional content related to our body recomposition coaching services

Message frequency varies based on your application stage and program enrollment. You may receive multiple messages per week during active enrollment periods.

Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier and plan.

Your consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchase. You may use our services without agreeing to receive promotional SMS by contacting us directly at karl@nobletransformation.com.

How to Opt Out: Reply STOP to any text message at any time to unsubscribe. After sending STOP, you will receive one final confirmation message. No further SMS messages will be sent to your number. To re-subscribe, reply START.

For Help: Reply HELP to any text message or email karl@nobletransformation.com.

Supported carriers: SMS service is available on all major U.S. carriers. Carrier support for short codes may vary.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party tools to operate our business (e.g., CRM software, email platforms, SMS messaging providers, scheduling tools). These providers are given access only to the information needed to perform their services and are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Noble Transformation LLC, our users, or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or a prominent notice on our website.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, maintain our coaching relationships, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Applicants who do not enroll in the program may have their data retained for up to 24 months for follow-up purposes.

If you wish to have your personal information deleted, please contact us at karl@nobletransformation.com and we will process your request within 30 days.

Your Rights & Choices

  • Opt Out of SMS: Reply STOP to any text message at any time
  • Opt Out of Email Marketing: Click the "Unsubscribe" link in any marketing email
  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you

To exercise any of these rights, email us at karl@nobletransformation.com with your request.
