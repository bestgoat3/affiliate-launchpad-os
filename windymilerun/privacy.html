Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Windymilerun is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We will never sell your personal data.

Who We Are

Windymilerun ("we," "our," or "us") is a weekly Saturday morning run club based in Chicago, Illinois. We operate at windymilerun.com and collect information through our waitlist and communication sign-up forms.

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: karl@windymile.run

Information We Collect

When you join our waitlist or sign up for SMS reminders, we collect:

  • Identity information: First name, last name
  • Contact information: Email address, phone number (if provided for SMS)
  • Location information: Neighborhood or area within Chicago (self-reported)
  • Discovery information: How you heard about Windymilerun
  • Technical information: UTM source and campaign parameters from the URL you visited

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or sensitive personal data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you confirmation emails after joining the waitlist
  • Notify you when registration opens (email)
  • Send Saturday morning run reminders via SMS (only if you opted in)
  • Send weekly route information and café location updates
  • Improve our community and event planning based on neighborhood data
  • Understand how people find us to improve our outreach

SMS Communications

If you opt in to SMS reminders, you will receive:

  • A Friday evening text with Saturday run details (meetup spot, route, café)
  • Occasional updates about the season (start, end, special events)

Message frequency: up to 2 messages per week during the June–October season. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. Reply HELP for assistance.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Kit (ConvertKit): Our email and SMS platform, which processes and stores subscriber data. Kit is GDPR-compliant. See Kit's privacy policy at kit.com/privacy.
  • Google LLC: We use Google Sheets to track waitlist registrations for operational purposes. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as you are an active member of our community or subscriber to our communications. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing karl@windymile.run. We will delete your data within 30 days of a verified request.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • File a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, email us at karl@windymile.run.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses URL parameters (UTM tags) to understand how visitors find us. We do not currently use cookies for tracking purposes. If this changes, we will update this policy and notify subscribers.

Children's Privacy

Windymilerun is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify active subscribers of material changes by email. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: karl@windymile.run
  • Website: windymilerun.com
