What Is Windymilerun

Where Morning People
Find Their People

Every Saturday from June 6th through the last Saturday of October, a group of Chicago's best morning people meets at 8AM for a 3-mile lakefront run. Music playing, strangers becoming friends, and coffee waiting at the end.

This isn't a race. There's no timer, no bib, no pressure. Just a great route, great energy, and the kind of community you actually want to be part of on a Saturday morning.
